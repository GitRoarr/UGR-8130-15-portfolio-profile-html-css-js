Biography

Hello, I'm Girma Enkuchile, born in 2004 in the beautiful town of Alem Ketema, North Shewa, Ethiopia. My childhood in this culturally rich and tightly-knit community instilled in me a love for learning and curiosity about the world. I began my academic journey at Arebegnoch Secondary School in Alem Ketema, where I laid a strong foundation in education.

Currently, I'm pursuing a degree in software engineering at the Addis Ababa Institute of Technology (AAiT), one of the top institutions in Ethiopia. My passion for technology drives me to explore innovative solutions in software development. I am committed to learning and applying my skills to solve real-world problems.
