About Me

Biography

Hello, I’m Girma Enkuchile, born in 2004 in the beautiful town of Alem Ketema, North Shewa, Ethiopia. My childhood in this culturally rich and tightly-knit community instilled in me a love for learning and curiosity about the world. I began my academic journey at Arebegnoch Secondary School in Alem Ketema, where I laid a strong foundation in education. Completing my preparatory education in the 2022/2023 academic year was a proud milestone that opened doors to exciting opportunities.

Currently, I’m pursuing a degree in software engineering at the Addis Ababa Institute of Technology (AAiT), one of the top institutions in Ethiopia. My passion for technology drives me to explore innovative solutions in software development. I am committed to learning and applying my skills to solve real-world problems. Beyond academics, I am deeply passionate about community development and aim to contribute to the growth and progress of my country. I believe in lifelong learning and continually strive to grow both personally and professionally to make a meaningful impact in the field of technology and beyond.
